Photography

Photography is all about capturing through different lenses to add creativity. Photography is an art, it is process of creating an image. According to my experience anything captured around us whether sunrise, sunset or nature scenes becomes a memory later. Every picture has a story to tell. Taking Photography as a hobby has been inspired by my family and friends. And since its one of trending passion nowadays. Photography is fun and fascinating process. You can dive in and start snapping away at anything that piques your interest.

There are some tips and skills about photography:

  • Expose and focus in object then frame your shot.
  • Makes lots of mistakes to learn from them.
  • Always be prepared, make sure to use wider aperture for portraits.
  • Make use of reflections especially during golden hours. These are great opportunities where people will not be able to capture it.
  • And the most important thing is avoid shaking the camera. Its all begins how you learn to hold camera perfectly.

Badminton

Playing badminton was my passion when I was 10 years till now. It is played with rackets and shuttlercock. There are 6 main shots: the serve, the clear, the drop, the smash, the forehand drive and backhand drive. The court on which badminton is played is in shape of rectangle and is divided in half by a net. When there are only 2 players one on each side of net it is single game and the double game where 2 on each side.

This game was first played in the home of Duke of Baufort, Badminton House in Gloucesteershire, England. This game can be played with any group age preferabbly 9 and above.

Benefits Of playing Badminton

  • Reduces stress.
  • Encourages mobility.
  • Helps to achieve optimum heart functions.
  • Increases flexibility and muscle strength.
  • Psychological Benefits.

Table Tennis

Table tennis is also known as ping-pong. This game was invented in England in early days of 20 th century. Professional table tennis is played on a hard table which is divided by a net. Players stand opposite to each other, and hit a lightweight ball with a small round bat across the table. Spinning the ball makes the chances of hitting the ball a lot less. So, players tend to spin the ball before serving it to the opponent. Table tennis is played as singles as well as doubles. It is played in both men’s and women’s category. Table tennis is played by the rules set by the International Table Tennis Federation (ITTF).

The purpose of this sport is to:

  • Improve the hand-eye coordination(concentration).
  • Improves reflexes.
  • Burns calories, makes you balance.
  • Keeps your brain sharp.
